# Legal & CA Sign-off

As of the current iteration, this engine is a **prototype** and has **NOT** received formal sign-off from a Chartered Accountant (CA) or a legal authority regarding its interpretation of the GST/IGST/CGST Acts.

## Pending Validation
- Statutory checks (e.g. Place of Supply, RCM) are implemented as mathematical constraints but require legal vetting.
- We require a `Contact >= 1` proof artifact from a certified CA before declaring the engine "production-ready".
